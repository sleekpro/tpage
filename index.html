Nelson R. Mandela

A stateman who build the nation on platform of his character

Here's the time line of Nelson Mandela's Life:

  • 1918 -Born at Mvezo in Transkei.
  • 1937 -Attends Healdtown, the Wesleyan College at Fort Beaufort.
  • 1939 -Enrols at University College of Fort Hare, in Alice.
  • 1942 -Completes BA through the University of South Africa (UNISA)
  • 1951 -Elected president of ANCYL.
  • 1962 -Leaves the country for military training and to garner support for the ANC.
  • 1982 -Mandela, Sisulu, Raymond Mhlaba and Andrew Mlangeni and later Ahmed Kathrada are sent to Pollsmoor Prison.
  • 1985 -Admitted to the Volks Hospital for prostate surgery.
  • 1988 -Admitted to Tygerberg Hospital where he is diagnosed with tuberculosis.
  • 1990 -ANC is unbanned.Elected ANC Deputy President.
  • 1993 -Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize with President FW de Klerk.
  • 1994 -Votes for the first time in his life.Elected by Parliament as first president of a democratic South Africa.Inaugurated as President of the Republic of South Africa.
    -Launches his autobiography Long Walk to Freedom.
  • 1995 -Establishes the Nelson Mandela Children's Fund.
  • 1996 -Divorces Winnie Mandela.
  • 1998 -Marries Graça Machel on his 80th birthday.
  • 2010 -Formally presented with the Fifa World Cup trophy before it embarks on a tour of South Africa.
  • 2013 -Passes away at home in Johannesburg.
